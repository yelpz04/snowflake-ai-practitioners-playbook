# Day 10: Snowflake CoWork Artifacts

## Goal
Generate rich, interactive outputs (charts, tables) from natural language and share them as Artifacts.

## References
- Artifacts are rich interactive outputs from Snowflake CoWork conversations

## What to Do
1. In Snowflake CoWork, ask questions that produce visualizations:
   - "Show revenue trends over the last 6 months" → Line Chart
   - "Compare revenue by product category" → Bar Chart
   - "What is the product category mix for won deals?" → Pie Chart
   - "Show discount patterns across regions" → Heatmap
   - "Is there a correlation between discount % and win rate?" → Scatter Plot
2. Save each output as an Artifact
3. Share one Artifact with a teammate
4. Document: what chart type was auto-selected for each question?

## Key Talking Points
- Artifacts = publication-ready deliverables, not screenshots
- AI picks the right visualization based on your question
- Shareable, interactive, connected to underlying data
- No need to rebuild in slides or BI tools

## Supported Chart Types
- 📈 Line Charts (trends)
- 📊 Bar Charts (comparisons)
- 🍩 Pie Charts (composition)
- 🔥 Heatmaps (patterns)
- 🔀 Scatter Plots (correlations)
